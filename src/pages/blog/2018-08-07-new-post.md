So, here we are.

I grew up outside of the city, before YouTube was a thing, before every new piece of gear came with a manual and a blog post and a lot of people who know what to do with it - or who've even heard of it. 

Over the last 15 years I've driven all around Ontario, buying, trading and selling gear. I'm going to try to put some of those stories and reviews here, even if for no other reason than to keep everything straight for myself.
